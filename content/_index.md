# Advancing the Art of Learning to Solder

Fellowship of the Flux is a Kansas City-based volunteer organization dedicated to making soldering education accessible to everyone. From cybersecurity conferences to school workshops, we bring hands-on learning experiences that build skills and community.

## Our Mission

We advance the art of learning to solder through:
- **Community Workshops** at maker faires, conferences, and STEM events
- **Portable Soldering Labs** with professional-grade equipment
- **Educational Resources** for individuals and organizations
- **Volunteer Training** to expand our reach and impact

## Community Impact

Since our founding, Fellowship of the Flux has:
- Taught hundreds of people to solder at community events
- Supported STEM education across the Midwest
- Provided resources for organizations to build their own programs
- Created a network of passionate volunteers and educators
