Learn to Solder

Welcome to your comprehensive soldering education hub. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to advance your skills, our structured learning path will guide you through every aspect of soldering.

Learning Paths

  • Beginner Path: Start with the basics and build your foundation
  • Intermediate Path: Develop advanced techniques and precision
  • Expert Path: Master complex projects and specialized applications

What You’ll Learn

  • Soldering fundamentals and safety
  • Tool selection and maintenance
  • Component identification and handling
  • Advanced techniques and troubleshooting
  • Project-based learning with real-world applications

Fellowship of the Flux

Fellowship of the Flux is a Kansas City-based volunteer organization advancing the art of learning to solder through community outreach, education, and resource sharing.
